Company Profile

PT Pelayaran Cahaya Nusantara

a private Indonesia shipping company, With the founder Mr. Wardono Asnim and Mr Poniman Asnim. With the passion in Shipping Business, Loving the Indonesia maritime and long experiences in ship engineering and ship building – wood and steel ship, PT PCN itself is a subsidiary of PT INDO SHIPPING GROUP with the mission to connect all islands of Indonesia and be part of logistic/distribution of Products all over Indonesia and also next level to become global shipping company. We can do all this because of the support and opportunity of all our customers and suppliers and great teams.

COMPANY’S VISION

Our Company Vision is to become the Outstanding Shipping Company in Indonesia utilizing well maintain ship and best human resources and best technology to do our shipping / logistic services

COMPANY’S MISSION

  • Connected all Indonesia Island and help industries distribute their cargo peaceful by one stop service concept.
  • We would raise many young generation leaders through our company.
  • Be the impact in the community through our work culture and ethics.
  • We would raise many great ship engineer through our work.
  • We would continuously train our seaman to become the best and raise their standard and skill and can be recognized globally.

Services

Currently we have 100 owned fleet to serve all Indonesia port Anderson also south east asia and we will expand to go Global.
We are also welcome ship owner’s vessels to be operated and managed by us and welcome for cooperation and partnership to expand in Shipping Business in Indonesia. We have professionals ship management, crewing and agency team as well.
